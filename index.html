Fundamental Articles

Article 1

Freedom & Equality

"All human beings are born free and equal in dignity and rights."

Article 4

No Slavery

"No one shall be held in slavery or servitude; slavery is prohibited in all forms."

Article 26

Right to Education

"Everyone has the right to education. Education shall be free."

Champions of Change

Meet the people who dedicated their lives to fighting injustice.

Anti-Slavery

Harriet Tubman

Born into slavery, she escaped and subsequently made some 13 missions to rescue approximately 70 enslaved people using the network of antislavery activists known as the Underground Railroad.

Racial Equality

Nelson Mandela

A South African anti-apartheid revolutionary who served as President of South Africa. He spent 27 years in prison for fighting against institutionalized racism and segregation.

Education

Malala Yousafzai

A Pakistani activist for female education and the youngest Nobel Prize laureate. She survived an assassination attempt for speaking out about girls' right to go to school.
